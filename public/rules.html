🃏 Texas Hold'em Rules

Each player is dealt 2 private hole cards. Five community cards are dealt face-up in three rounds (Flop, Turn, River). Make the best 5-card hand using any combination of your 2 hole cards and the 5 community cards.
  • BLINDS The two players to the left of the dealer post the Small Blind and Big Blind before any cards are dealt.
  • DEAL Each player receives 2 face-down hole cards. A round of betting begins with the player left of the Big Blind (pre-flop).
  • FLOP Three community cards are dealt face-up. A betting round follows starting left of the dealer button.
  • TURN A fourth community card is dealt. Another betting round follows.
  • RIVER The fifth and final community card is dealt. A final betting round follows.
  • SHOWDOWN If two or more players remain after the final bet, hole cards are revealed. The best 5-card hand wins the pot.
  • BETTING Actions in order: Check (no bet open), Call (match current bet), Raise (increase bet), Fold (discard hand), All-In (bet all remaining chips).
  • SIDE POT When a player goes All-In and others have more chips, a side pot is created. The all-in player can only win the main pot.

🃏 Pot-Limit Omaha (PLO) Rules

Each player is dealt 4 hole cards. You must use exactly 2 of your 4 hole cards and exactly 3 of the 5 community cards to make your best hand.
  • KEY RULE Unlike Hold'em, you must use exactly 2 hole cards — no more, no less. You cannot play the board.
  • POT LIMIT The maximum bet at any time is the current size of the pot. Pre-flop, the max raise is based on the big blind amount.
  • BETTING Structure is identical to Hold'em: Blinds → Flop → Turn → River → Showdown.
  • NUTS With 4 hole cards there are 6 possible 2-card combinations. Always consider all 6 to find your best hand.
  • HAND RANKS Same hand rankings as Texas Hold'em apply.

💰 Buy-In & Cash-Out Rules

  • BUY-IN Minimum buy-in is set per table. You may sit with any amount up to the table maximum. New players start with 0 chips — admin adds chips after identity verification.
  • REBUY You may rebuy at any time when it is not your turn to act. Rebuys are processed by the admin/host.
  • ADD-ON You may top up your stack between hands. Request chips from admin/host via the "Need Chips?" section in the lobby.
  • CASH-OUT Use the Leave Table button at any time. Chips are returned to your account instantly. Contact admin to convert chips to cash.
  • PAYMENT All chip-to-cash conversions are handled by admin. Payment methods are arranged privately. Always verify your balance before leaving.
  • SHORT STACK A player with fewer chips than the big blind may go all-in. They may only win up to their all-in amount from each other player (side pots apply).

🏆 High Hand Jackpot Rules

The High Hand Jackpot rewards the best hand made during the jackpot period. The jackpot resets periodically and accumulates rake contributions between resets.
  • ELIGIBILITY Any hand using both hole cards and at least 3 community cards at showdown qualifies. The hand must reach showdown (no uncontested wins).
  • QUALIFYING HANDS Typically Four of a Kind or better qualifies — the qualifying threshold is set by admin.
  • AWARD The jackpot is awarded by the admin/host after verifying the qualifying hand. The winning player receives the full jackpot balance.
  • RESET The jackpot resets on a scheduled timer (visible in the lobby). A portion of each pot's rake feeds the jackpot.
  • DISPUTES All jackpot awards are final once confirmed by admin. Screenshots/hand history are reviewed for verification.
  • SUBMIT Hosts and players with qualifying hands should use the 🏆 High Hand button at the table to submit for review.

☕ Breaks & Timeout Rules

  • BREAK Each player is entitled to a limited number of breaks per session. Use the ☕ Break button at the table. Your seat is held while on break.
  • BREAK LIMIT Break passes are limited (typically 3 per session). Once break passes are exhausted, you will be marked as sitting out.
  • SHOT CLOCK When it's your turn to act, a timer runs. Failure to act before the timer expires results in an automatic fold (or check if no bet is open).
  • SITTING OUT If you go sitting-out (away), your hand will be folded each round. Antes/blinds are still posted. Return promptly to avoid being removed from the table.
  • SESSION TIMEOUT Players are automatically logged out after 2 hours of inactivity. Admins after 4 hours. A 5-minute warning is shown before logout.
  • DISCONNECT If you disconnect, your hand plays out normally (auto-fold on your turn). Reconnecting within a reasonable time restores your seat.

🤝 Player Conduct

  • RESPECT All players must treat each other and staff with respect. Abusive language in chat is not tolerated.
  • COLLUSION Soft-playing, chip dumping, or any form of collusion results in immediate removal and forfeiture of funds.
  • SLOW ROLL Deliberately delaying the reveal of a winning hand at showdown is considered poor etiquette.
  • ONE PLAYER One player per account. Sharing accounts or playing multiple accounts simultaneously is prohibited.
  • CHAT Keep table chat game-related. No politics, harassment, or spam. Admin can and will mute or remove players.
  • DECISIONS FINAL Once an action is submitted (fold, call, raise) it is final and binding. Misclicks are not reversed.
