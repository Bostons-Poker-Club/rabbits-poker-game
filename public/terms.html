SMS Terms & Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2025

Program Description

Program name: RabbsRoom Boston Poker Club
Description: Private poker club SMS notifications for buy-ins, game alerts, and table updates.

By providing your mobile phone number and opting in to receive SMS messages from RabbsRoom Boston Poker Club, you agree to receive recurring text messages related to poker game activity, including seat availability, buy-in confirmations, game start alerts, and table updates.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on game activity. You may receive more messages during active game nights and fewer messages during quiet periods.

Opt-Out Instructions

To stop receiving messages at any time, reply STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent to your number.

You may re-enroll at any time by contacting support.

Help & Support

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us by email at rogeriosdiningexperience@gmail.com.

Costs & Carrier Disclaimer

Message and data rates may apply. Standard SMS and data rates charged by your mobile carrier apply to messages sent and received. RabbsRoom Boston Poker Club does not charge for the SMS service itself.

Supported carriers include but are not limited to: AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, Sprint, Boost Mobile, MetroPCS, and other US carriers. Carrier support may vary.

Privacy

Your mobile phone number and personal information will not be shared with, sold to, or used by third parties for marketing purposes. Information collected is used solely to send you RabbsRoom Boston Poker Club game notifications.

We collect and store only the phone number you provide for SMS delivery. You may request removal of your information at any time by contacting rogeriosdiningexperience@gmail.com.

Eligibility

This SMS program is intended for members of RabbsRoom Boston Poker Club who have been invited and registered by an authorized club administrator. Membership is private and by invitation only.

Contact

  • SMS Opt-Out: Reply STOP to any message
